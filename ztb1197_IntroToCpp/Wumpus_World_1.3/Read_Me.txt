To go up type w or north. To go down type s or south.
To go left type west or a. To go right type east or d.

Avoid the Wumpus and pitfalls to find the gold and become rich or something.

Stepping outside the confines of the Wumpus' home will cause you to fall into
 the poison swamp so be wary of that too.

When you complete the game, either through finding the gold or dying, the game 
 will ask you if you want to play again type 'y' for yes or 'n' for no
